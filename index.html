WHY CHOOSE BIOIDENTICAL OVER SYNTHETIC HORMONES:

One fundamental distinction in hormone replacement therapy (HRT) is the choice between bioidentical and synthetic hormones. Bioidentical hormones are structurally identical to the hormones produced naturally by our body. They are a primary choice for prescribers when synthetic hormones are not suitable or when the compatibility and similarity to your own hormones are more appropriate, to minimise potential side effects and risks.

At My Compounding Pharmacy, we offer both synthetic and bioidentical hormone medication. As a specialty, our customisable compounded bioidentical hormones can be made in a delivery method that is suitable for you.

Topical Creams and Gels:

Easy to apply and may take time to be absorbed through the skin. Daily application may be less convenient for some patients.

Transdermal Patches:

Patches are another method for continuous hormone delivery and avoid first pass liver metabolism. While not all hormones are suitable in patch form, are visible and require rotation of application site. They may be a suitable form for those unable to swallow and require a single hormone replacement. Monitor for skin irritation or allergies.

Sublingual Drops / Lozenges:

Drops are administered under the tongue for rapid absorption and useful for patient’s who have difficulty with oral medications.

Suppositories:

Some patients may opt for suppositories short term when oral use may not be suitable.

Injections:

Provide a rapid absorption of hormone. Require a healthcare professional to administer. Useful for patient’s, who have difficulty with oral medications and require less frequent administration of some hormones.

Pellet Implants:

Provide continuous hormone release over several months, reducing a need for daily administration. They require a minor surgical procedure for insertion, difficult to adjust dosing once implanted and pose a risk to infection or extrusion at the implant site.

WHY CHOOSE A TROCHES FOR YOUR HRT METHOD:

  1. Precise Dosing: Troches allow for precise dosing adjustments, making it easier to customise your treatment.
  2. Absorption: Troches are designed to dissolve slowly in the mouth, allowing for direct absorption of hormones through the mucous membranes of the mouth, bypassing the digestive system and avoiding first-pass metabolism in the liver.
  3. Ease of Administration: Troches are typically easy for patients to administer themselves, as they simply dissolve in the mouth without the need for swallowing pills or injections.
  4. Convenient for Patients: Patients who have difficulty swallowing pills or who prefer not to use topical creams or gels may find troches to be a convenient alternative.
  5. Steady Hormone Release: Troches provide a consistent release of hormones over time, helping to maintain stable hormone levels.
  6. Reduced Risk of Skin Transfer: Unlike topical creams or gels, troches do not carry the risk of transferring hormones to others through skin contact.
  7. Taste and Sensation: Custom flavouring allows the taste or texture of troches more pleasant to take
  8. Frequency of Use: Troches are typically administered once-daily or more frequently depending on a person’s dosing requirements
  9. Storage and Stability: Troches may have specific storage requirements, such as refrigeration, to maintain their stability and effectiveness
  10. Compliance: Some patients may find taking troches an easy way to remember their medication as opose to taking capsules or applying creams at the required intervals, potentially leading to fluctuations in hormone levels.

BENEFITS OF HORMONE REPLACEMENT THERAPY

HRT offers a multitude of benefits, enhancing your overall quality of life. These advantages include:

  • Relief from Menopausal Symptoms: HRT effectively alleviates hot flashes, mood swings, and sleep disturbances.
  • Improved Energy Levels: Patients often experience a renewed sense of vitality and reduced fatigue.
  • Enhanced Mood: HRT can stabilise mood swings and reduce irritability.
  • Better Bone Health: Hormone replacement supports bone density and reduces the risk of osteoporosis.
  • Enhanced Cognitive Function: Some patients report improved cognitive function and mental clarity.
